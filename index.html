About Francolin College

Our History

Founded in 1995, Francolin College has been at the forefront of providing quality education for over 25 years. What started as a small institution with just 50 students has now grown into one of the most prestigious colleges in the region.

Our Mission

To provide transformative education that equips students with knowledge, skills, and values to excel in their chosen fields and contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that nurtures innovative thinkers and responsible global citizens.

Core Values

Admission Process

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Official transcripts from previous school
  • Two recommendation letters
  • Birth certificate
  • Medical report
  • Passport photographs
  • Entrance examination results

Important Dates

  • Application opens: January 15
  • Application deadline: March 30
  • Entrance examination: April 15
  • Interview dates: April 20-25
  • Admission decisions: May 10
  • Orientation: September 1
